About CareSync

Designed for Cipla Palliative Care

CareSync was developed from a need to reduce administrative overhead at the Cipla Palliative Care center. By unifying critical tasks into a single web application, we empower staff to work more efficiently and dedicate more time to compassionate patient interaction.

  • Centralized task management.
  • Reduced manual data entry.
  • Improved operational transparency.
